Admission Process

Primary School Entry

  • Basic 1 (Age 5-6)
  • Transfer admission for Basic 2-6
  • Entrance assessment test
  • Parent interview

Secondary School Entry

  • JSS 1 entry examination
  • Transfer admission for JSS 2-3
  • SSS 1-3 placement test
  • Previous academic records

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records
  • Immunization records
  • Passport photographs
  • Transfer certificate (for transfer students)
